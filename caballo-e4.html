About:

"10 Things I Hate About You" centers on the relationship between two high school students, Kat Stratford and Patrick Verona. Kat is a strong-willed, independent girl with a bit of a rebellious streak, while Patrick is the mysterious new guy.

The story follows Kat’s younger sister, Bianca, who wants to date but is grounded by their strict father, who won't allow her to date until Kat does. To solve this, Bianca's suitor, Cameron, enlists Patrick to date Kat, hoping it will allow Bianca to go out.

As Patrick and Kat begin to connect, their relationship develops in unexpected ways, leading to themes of love, identity, and self-acceptance. The film blends romance and comedy with a clever script, making it a beloved classic.
